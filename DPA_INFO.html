What juristictional acts are is in place for us today?

There are 2 main acts in place today designed to protect the general public from an exposure of their sensitive data:

  1. Data Protection act
  2. General Data protection Regulation

Data Protection:

The data protection act was originally created in 1998 and was revisied and reintroduced in 2018 this is soley made for the uk and controls the restricts the use of personal data by companys and keeps sensitive data safe.

General Data Protection Regulation:

This was originally brought in under the Eu regulations as a EU wide Data Protection Act and as the UK split from the EU it was adapted into the UK's own DPA.

THE Information Commisioner

The Information Commissioner is an independent official appointed by the Crown. The Commissioner's decisions are subject to appeal to an independent tribunal and the courts. The Commissioner's mission is to "uphold information rights in the public interest, promoting openness by public bodies and data privacy for individuals.
